My Web Development Journey

Prelude

I was introduced to the basics of HTML in 2021 during my Senior High School days. I gained admission to Ghana Communication Technology University in 2024, where I was exposed to the depth of web programming and design. Mr. Danso Joseph, my tutor for web programming, introduced me to FreeCodeCamp, a free and responsive website for learning how to create websites, grooming us for the industrial world of web programming and design.
